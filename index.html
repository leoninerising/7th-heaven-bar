What’s Cooking at the FF7 Remake Cafe?

The long-awaited FF7 remake is finally coming, and to celebrate, Square Enix Cafe Tokyo and Osaka, as well as ARTNIA, is dishing out a special FF7-themed menu! The Final Fantasy VII Remake Cafe begins in 2020, and will be the first collab cafe at the Square Enix Cafe Tokyo since its renovation. Since it’s happening over a couple of months, the menu will change partially, so that it might be worth checking out a second time.

Food & Desserts

FF7-themed food! These below will be available throughout the whole event. Lots of character references here.

  • Entire Event

  1. Moogle Sweet Bean Bun
  2. The Flower That Only Blooms Here Fruit Parfait
  3. The Flower Girl’s Salad

Apparently the Yakiniku Set is one of those items you can eat within in the Wall Market location of the game - they give you a screenshot from the original game to remind you. The Risotto is based on the redness of Reno’s (one of their many characters) hair. And of course – Materia soup dumplings, anyone? (Will I be able to cast a spell if I eat one….?)

  • First Round

  1. Yakiniku Set
  2. Risotto
  3. Materia Xiao Long Bao

And finally, these will come in to replace the ones above for the second round. The Sashimi Set is in a similar vein as the Yakiniku Set in the previous round. It seems like Wall Market also sold Yakisoba. Seventh Heaven’s Eggs & Chips are here for your tasting, and a cute little Moogle dessert will also be available.

  • Second Round

  1. Sashimi Set
  2. Wall Market’s Yakisoba
  3. Eggs & Chips
  4. Moogle Sweet Mousse
